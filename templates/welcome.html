CREATE TABLE users (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY,
  username TEXT UNIQUE,
  email TEXT,
  created_at DATETIME DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP
);

SELECT * FROM users;

-- Blazing fast queries at the edge
SELECT
  COUNT(*) as total_users,
  strftime('%Y-%m', created_at) as month
FROM users
GROUP BY month
ORDER BY month DESC;

Our Manifesto

Databases for people, not profit

We believe in putting the power of data back in the hands of developers.

Simplicity Over Complexity

We believe that databases should be simple to set up, use, and maintain. SQLite embodies this philosophy, and we've built SQLWave to preserve this simplicity while adding cloud-scale capabilities.

Data Sovereignty

Your data belongs to you, not us. We provide the tools to manage, store, and access your data efficiently, but you maintain complete ownership and control at all times.

Transparent and Predictable Pricing

No surprise bills, no hidden fees. We believe in transparent pricing that scales with your usage, so you can build with confidence knowing your costs will remain predictable.

Open Standards and Interoperability

We embrace open standards and ensure your databases remain portable. No vendor lock-in means you can always export your data and schema in standard formats.

Speed as a Feature

We believe that database performance directly impacts user experience. Our edge network ensures your queries are executed as close to your users as possible, minimizing latency.

Developer Experience First

Every feature, API, and tool we build starts with the developer experience. We optimize for joy, productivity, and intuitive interfaces at every step.
